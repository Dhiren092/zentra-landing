Questions, answered.

Is Zentra built for Mauritius specifically?

Yes. Zentra handles MUR currency, NPF, CSG, and PAYE calculations out of the box. VAT reporting is built in. All defaults are set for Mauritian businesses from day one.

How long does setup take?

Most businesses are live within a week. We import your products, staff list, and suppliers for you. You don't need an IT team — if you can use WhatsApp, you can use Zentra.

What happens to my data if I cancel?

Your data is yours. You can export everything — sales history, staff records, inventory — as a CSV at any time. We keep your data for 90 days after cancellation, then delete it securely.

Does it work offline or if the internet cuts?

The POS continues to process sales offline. Transactions queue locally and sync automatically when your connection returns. You'll never lose a sale because of a network drop.

Can I try it before committing to a plan?

Yes. Every plan starts with a free trial — no credit card required. You get full access to every module. Only pay when you're confident it works for your business.

Who do I call if something breaks?

We're reachable on WhatsApp during business hours. You can also book a call directly with one of the founders — we're a small team and we take support seriously.
